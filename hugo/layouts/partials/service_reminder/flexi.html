Flexi Service Plan

Flexi Service Plan is a tailor-made scheduled service plan where you can choose from variety of options. At any point of ownership period, you can purchase a Flexi Plan, which covers the costs oil change services and labour at a savings of up to 25%. And coverage is 100% tranferable, increasing the resale value of your car.
